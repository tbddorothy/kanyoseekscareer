About the Initiative

Dimpho Mahoko who is the founde of this initiative was getting to a point where her younger siblings and cousins needed to answer questions about their career plans but did not have enough information to make an informed decision. Some of them made the right choice, others just chose something to avoid having chose nothing. So we decided to make this site for people to be able to have a peak at what their career could be from real people, in the real industry
